DESTINATIONS NOT TO BE MISSED

KOTA DEPOK

STUDIO ALAM TVRI
Studio Alam TVRI is an artificial forest area located in Sukmajaya, Depok, and is one of the unique natural tourism destinations in this region.

Initially, this place was built as a filming location for movies and TV shows produced by TVRI. Now, Studio Alam TVRI has become a tourist spot offering green natural scenery, a serene atmosphere, and various interesting activities.

Studio Alam TVRI is perfect for those seeking a peaceful ambiance, fresh air, and interaction with nature without needing to travel far from the city. Whether for family recreation, sports, or simply relaxing, Studio Alam TVRI provides an authentic natural experience with a traditional Indonesian feel.

  • Address : Jalan Raden Saleh, Sukmajaya, Depok.
  • Entrance Fee : Rp 5,000 per person.
  • Operating Hours : Daily from 07:30 to 16:00 WIB.

SETU BABAKAN
Setu Babakan is one of the cultural and natural tourism destinations located in Jagakarsa, South Jakarta, near the border with Depok. Setu Babakan is famous as the Betawi Cultural Village Center, where visitors can enjoy various aspects of Betawi community life and traditions, such as art, cuisine, and customs, while appreciating the natural beauty of the surroundings.

Setu Babakan is an artificial lake spanning about 30 hectares. The lake serves as a center for water recreation and is a main attraction due to its cool and lush atmosphere.

Setu Babakan functions as a preservation site for Betawi culture. Visitors can see and learn about various Betawi traditions, including traditional Betawi houses made of wood and adorned with traditional ornaments that have become iconic in this area.

At Setu Babakan, visitors can enjoy Betawi cultural performances while savoring authentic Betawi dishes.

  • Address : Jalan Situ Babakan, Sukmajaya, Depok
  • Entrance Fee : Free
  • Operating Hours : Daily from 09:00 to 15:00 WIB

MASJID DIAN AL-MAHRI
The Dian Al-Mahri Mosque, also known as the Golden Dome Mosque, is one of the iconic religious tourism sites located in Limo, Depok, West Java. The mosque is famous for its grandeur, especially its domes plated with pure gold, as well as its beautiful and luxurious architecture. In addition to being a place of worship, the mosque is also a popular religious tourism destination that attracts many visitors from various regions.

The mosque has five domes, consisting of one main dome and four smaller domes, all coated with 24-carat pure gold. The architectural style of the mosque adopts Middle Eastern design, with rich ornaments and intricate details. Inside the mosque, a large crystal chandelier imported from Italy further enhances the sense of luxury.

Situated on a land area of about 50 hectares, the mosque features neatly arranged gardens and courtyards. The green and well-maintained gardens provide a relaxing atmosphere for visitors to unwind after worship.

The Golden Dome Mosque is a perfect blend of a place of worship, architectural beauty, and tourist attraction. Its majestic golden domes and sacred ambiance make it a must-visit destination, whether for worship, religious tourism, or simply admiring the beauty of Islamic architecture.

  • Address : Jalan Raya Meruyung, Limo, Depok
  • Entrance Fee : Free
  • Operating Hours : Daily
